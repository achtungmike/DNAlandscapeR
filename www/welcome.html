About

We designed DNAlandscapeR to provide a visualization of epigenetic data, particularly three-dimensional chromatin structures from ChIA-PET and HiC experiments, in a computationally efficient framework. These tracks can be viewed immediately alongside other epigenetic tracks, such as DNA methylation, transcription binding intensities, and RNA expression.

Authors

Caleb Lareau is the creator/developer of DNAlandscapeR as a graduate student under the supervision of Martin Aryee, PhD.

First time user?

Try loading some sample data by first clicking the button at the bottom of the Visualize tab. Afterwards, hit "Plot Region" to visualize these data in the browser window. Also, check out the Guide tab for a better overview of the features of the DNAlandscapeR browser.
